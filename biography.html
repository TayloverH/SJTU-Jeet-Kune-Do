协会简介

上海交通大学截拳道协会前身是散打协会，成立于2001年。协会旨在通过学习李小龙的功夫，了解李小龙的哲学思想，追随李小龙的奋斗精神，来增强身体素质，活跃辩证思维，培养吃苦品格。截拳道协会遵循李小龙“以无法为有法，以无限为有限”的名言，鼓励同学们不怕失败，超越自我，以不馁的精神迎接生活的挑战。

Introduction

The Jeet Kune Do association of Shanghai Jiao Tong University, whose precursor was the Kick-boxing association, was formed in 2001. The association is aimed at helping its members gain strength, develop critical thinking ability and be able to bear hardship by learning the Kungfu , philosophic thinking and the striving spirit of Bruce Lee’s. By following Bruce Lee’s famous saying that “In the absence of law, the limit is infinite. ”, our association encourages its members to get rid of the fear of failure, surpass themselves and face the challenges in life with persistence.
